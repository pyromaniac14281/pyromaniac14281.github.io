The Rules

There are 5 questions, you have 50 seconds to answer all 5 questions.

1. If there is a basket of 13 apples and you take three of them, how many apples do you have?





2. How many 2-cent stamps does it take to make a dozen?





3. Mary’s father has 5 daughters – Nana, Nene, Nini, Nono. What is the fifth daughters name?





4. What travels around the world but stays in one spot?





4. If you divide 14 by 1/2, what do you get?
